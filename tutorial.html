Tutorials

Card spreads are the different ways tarot readers lay out their cards and then interpret the card meanings. In addition to the many types of tarot decks available, there are also a variety of ways to lay out cards. The Celtic Cross is the most popular spread. Other spreads include but are not limited to: Tetraktys, Astrological Spread, Cross and Triangle, and Tree of Life Spread.
