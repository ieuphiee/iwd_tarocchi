Ask the Cards

Helpful Tips & Guidelines

  • Before selecting a card, try to think of an open ended question that you want to ask the cards.
  • You can ask the cards a question about the past, present, or future.
  • Try to frame the question in a way that provides space for interpretation, rather than just a simple "yes" or "no" question.
  • Your question should focus on yourself rather than on others. This is about you and your decisions.
  • Examples of good questions include: How can I improve...? or What do I need to know about...?
  • If you're ready, select a card below.
